# Client Case Study Generator

A one-page HTML generator for creating client marketing case studies from campaign numbers.

## What it does

- Lets you enter raw before/after ad account numbers.
- Calculates CPA reduction from before and after CPA.
- Calculates conversion rate, CTR, ROAS, cost per qualified lead, and lead lift.
- Shows a polished case-study preview inspired by the provided template.
- Lets you edit strategy bullets and key takeaway copy.
- Downloads the final case study as Word-compatible `.doc`, standalone `.html`, or print/save as PDF.

## Numbers to collect for each client

- Timeframe in days
- Before and after ad spend
- Before and after results, conversions, or leads
- Before and after conversion value or revenue
- Before and after link clicks
- Before and after impressions
- Before and after qualified leads from CRM or sales review
- Optional: ad relevance, quality ranking, or quality score

## Use

Open `index.html` in a browser, enter the client's raw ad account numbers, review the calculated case-study metrics, then download the document.
